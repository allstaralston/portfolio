ABOUT ME

Gabrielle Alston is a technical writer by profession, creative writer by habit. She has been published in both realms; primarily journalistic and research publications but also poetry collections. The research publications focused on the city of Atlantis, Leprechauns, and the Loch Ness monster: technical writing or creative writing, you decide.
Gabrielle Alston is a graduate of Saginaw Valley State University. With a degree in Professional and Technical Writing as well as minors in Creative Writing and Spanish, language is at the heart of everything that she does whether for business or pleasure.
To view her work, feel free to explore this site, or you can visit this author on LinkedIn, Facebook, or Instagram under Gabrielle Alston.
